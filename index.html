Fiverr and Upwork Are Crowded. You’re Fighting in a Pool of Sharks.

Trying to get clients on Fiverr, Upwork, and other freelance platforms can feel like fighting in a pool of sharks.

Thousands of freelancers are bidding for the same jobs. Some already have hundreds of reviews. Some are ready to charge very low prices just to win the project. As a beginner or growing freelancer, it can be hard to stand out.

But here is the truth: many businesses outside those platforms need websites, SEO, social media management, copywriting, branding, ads, video editing, and other digital services. They may not be posting jobs on freelance platforms.

That is where direct outreach comes in.
